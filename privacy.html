Privacy Policy

Effective 23 July 2026. CaribbeanSaaS is independently curated by Caynetic Ltd. This policy explains the information connected with this website and how to contact us about it.

Information we receive

When you email us, we receive the information you choose to include, such as your name, email address, product details, and correction or removal requests. Our hosting and security providers may process technical request information such as IP address, browser or device details, and page requests to deliver and protect the website.

Catalog information

We research and publish public-facing information about products, companies, and teams to operate the directory and its public catalog data. We aim to publish only public-safe business and product information. Do not send sensitive personal information in a listing request. If a listing is inaccurate or you are authorized to request a correction or removal, email us at hello@caribbeansaas.com.

Cookies and third parties

CaribbeanSaaS does not load advertising or analytics scripts. The website uses services needed to deliver and present it, including its hosting/CDN, Google Fonts, and Tailwind’s browser-delivered stylesheet. Those providers may process technical request information under their own policies. Our site also links to independently operated product websites, which have their own privacy practices.

How we use and share information

We use contact and catalog information to review submissions, maintain accurate listings, respond to requests, operate the public catalog, and protect the website. We may share information with service providers that help operate the site or email service, or where required to comply with law, protect rights, or address security issues. We do not use email submissions to create a public user profile.

Retention and choices

We retain information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes above, to maintain the curation record, or to meet legal and operational obligations. You may ask us to access, correct, or remove personal information that you have provided, subject to applicable law and legitimate recordkeeping needs. Browser controls can manage many cookies and site-data preferences.

Updates and contact

We may update this policy when the website, its data practices, or applicable requirements change. The effective date above will be updated with any material revision. For privacy questions or requests, contact hello@caribbeansaas.com.
